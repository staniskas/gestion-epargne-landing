Frequently asked questions

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). Open your device Settings → [Apple ID / Google Account] → Subscriptions to view or cancel.

I need a refund.
Refund requests are handled by Apple or Google. Please contact their customer support with your purchase details.
