# Legal pages (Terms & Privacy)

1. **Replace placeholders** in `terms.html` and `privacy.html`:
   - `legal@example.com` and `privacy@example.com` → your real contact emails.

2. **Host the `legal/` folder** so both pages and `legal.css` are served:
   - Your own domain: e.g. `https://yourdomain.com/legal/terms.html`, `https://yourdomain.com/legal/privacy.html`
   - GitHub Pages, Netlify, or any static hosting: upload the folder and use the generated URLs.

3. **Use these URLs** in your RevenueCat (or Superwall) paywall as the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy links.
